ABOUT ME

My name is Daniela De Simone, and I am from Buenos Aires, Argentina. I graduated in UBA (Universidad de Buenos Aires) and got my medical degree. Then, I discovered my passion in designing and developing web and mobile pages and apps.

I started studying web developing on Academlo and Open Bootcamp, where I learned to use JS, HTML, CSS, REACT and GIT. Since then, I have been developing multiple personal and class projects, always looking for the most original way to design them.

Nowadays, I try to gather the skills that both of my careers gave me: my critical thinking and creativity, and use them in creating digital solutions for my clients. I love investigating, learning, and working on new challenges and goals in order to become every day a better person and professional.
